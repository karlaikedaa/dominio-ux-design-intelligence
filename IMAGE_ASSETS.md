## Image Requirements

**Video Thumbnails:**
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 (1280x720px or 1920x1080px)
- Format: JPG
- Quality: 80-85% compression

**Diagrams/Illustrations:**
- Format: PNG (supports transparency)
- Quality: Lossless compression
- Dimensions: Variable (maintain readable resolution)
